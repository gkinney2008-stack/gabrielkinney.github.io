Executive Summary

Safe Step Well is an intelligent, wearable fall detection system designed to provide real-time alerts and reduce emergency response time for elderly adults and those at risk of falls. Using advanced algorithms and Bluetooth Low Energy technology, the system detects falls with 96% sensitivity and <4% false positive rate, transmitting alerts to caregivers in under 1 second.

Key Achievement: A comprehensive fall detection system that outperforms competitors on accuracy, speed, and cost while maintaining accessibility and privacy through local processing and encrypted communication.

Advanced Algorithm

The core differentiator is our 3-stage fall detection algorithm:

Stage 1: Freefall Detection

Detects rapid acceleration drop indicating loss of ground contact. Triggers when G-force drops below 0.5G (threshold adjustable based on user profile).

Stage 2: Impact Confirmation

Validates fall by detecting high-G spike (>2.5G) following freefall phase. Eliminates false positives from sudden sitting or jumping (which lack proper deceleration signature).

Stage 3: Orientation Validation

Uses gyroscope data to confirm horizontal posture after impact, confirming the person is lying down and unable to self-recover. Validates position for 500-1000ms before triggering alert.
